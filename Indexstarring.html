Index (Starring John The Radio Presenter)


Digital video

6 min. Loop

2020


Index (Starring John the Radio Presenter) is a video consisting of various images and clips of ‘camouflaged’ networks and infrastructural systems; stealth cell towers in the form of trees, electric transformer houses disguised as private homes, antennas painted to match fasades, CCTV cameras covered in bark and a stone housing water pipes. Alongside the images is a voice over read by a radio presenter describing a room he’s positioned in; a seemingly constant loop of ruptures between inside/outside, private and public.
